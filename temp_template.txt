The elderly are confronted with problems such as irregular medication, forgetting to measure blood pressure and blood glucose, and difficulties in accessing health knowledge. Meanwhile, their children are eager to keep track of their parents' health status. Based on the AI-Native concept, this project designs and develops an intelligent health assistant system for the elderly. Adopting natural language dialogue interaction, the system realizes core functions including medication recording, health data management, active reminders and health consultation. It effectively lowers the barriers for the elderly to use health management tools and provides a way for children to remotely care for their parents. This paper elaborates on the design ideas and implementation schemes of the silver-haired health assistant from the perspectives of demand analysis, system design and technical implementation.Key wordsIntelligent health assistant; elderly health management; AI-Native; natural language processing; active service引言论文目标与定位1.1.1 老年人健康管理痛点通过实地调研和文献分析，我们发现老年人在健康管理方面存在以下主要问题：1.用药依从性低：由于记忆力衰退、认知能力下降，老年人经常忘记按时服药或重复服药2.健康监测困难：血压、血糖等健康指标需要定期测量，但老年人容易忘记或不知道如何记录3.健康知识获取不便：老年人对互联网信息辨别能力有限，难以获取可靠的健康知识4.子女关爱缺失：子女工作繁忙，无法及时了解父母健康状况，存在安全隐患1.2
